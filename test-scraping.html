Simple Pancakes Recipe

This is a test recipe for scraping functionality.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat eggs and add milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Don't overmix.
  4. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat. Cook pancakes for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with syrup and your favorite toppings.
